Musashi Miyamoto was a samurai that lived during the Edo period in Japan and is most notable for the niten ichi ryu, or the practice of using a long sword and a short sword at once in combat. Nice Block 1. His exploits and duels have earned him a place as a saint of swordsmanship in the canon of martial arts, and the most famous novelization of his life, written by Eiji Yoshikawa, has been adapted into films and manga, watched and read by people around the globe
